Raycast Extensions & Hotkeys
=============================
Import via: Raycast > Settings > Advanced > Export/Import

Window Management
- Almost Maximize: Option X
- Left Half: Option ,
- Make Larger: Option +
- Make Smaller: Option -
- Maximize: Option F
- Right Half: Option .

Applications
- ChatGPT Atlas: Option A
- Notes: Option Q
- Notion Calendar: Option C
- Obsidian: Option O

Clipboard History: Option V
